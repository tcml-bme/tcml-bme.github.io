This work was in collaboration with [Prof. Ram Weiss](https://www.rambam.org.il/en/?catid=%7B2401e4ad-b260-4af6-a018-ca187d14c9ed%7D&itemid=%7B8f3f76ef-4f44-43b9-aa23-d69a3d9c5494%7D) from the Rambam Health Care Campus.
