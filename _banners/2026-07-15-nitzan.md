Congratulations to **Nitzan Avidan-Pearl** on her new paper, accepted to the journal [Medical & Biological Engineering & Computing](https://link.springer.com/journal/11517). The paper shows how neural networks can improve the repeatability of pancreatic IVIM MRI analysis. The work was conducted at the [Technion Faculty of Biomedical Engineering](https://bme.technion.ac.il/) and the [May-Blum-Dahl Human MRI Research Center](https://techmrc.technion.ac.il/), in collaboration with Dr. Daphna Link-Sourani and Prof. Ram Weiss
