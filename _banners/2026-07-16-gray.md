Congratulations! We are honored to receive a **[Gray Foundation](https://www.grayfoundation.org/)** Science grant to advance AI-powered MRI for the early detection of ovarian cancer. The work brings together the Technion, University of Pennsylvania, and University of Chicago, with parts carried out at the Technion’s [May-Blum-Dahl Human MRI Research Center](https://techmrc.technion.ac.il/) and the Technion Computational MRI Lab.
